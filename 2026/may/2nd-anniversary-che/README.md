# Namma Flutter — Panju Anniversary

**Vaanga Parakalaam. Siragadikka Aasai 😌**

**Date:** 9th May 2026

**Location:** Comcast India Engineering Center I LLP, Thoraipakkam, Chennai, Tamil Nadu

**Type:** Community Event

Namma Flutter turns 2! A full-day community celebration filled with learning, sharing, networking, and growing the Flutter ecosystem together.

What better way to celebrate than coming together as a community to share knowledge, build connections, and make Flutter stronger.
